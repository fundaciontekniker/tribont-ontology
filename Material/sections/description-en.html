Material module: Description back to ToC

The Material class represents the elements of the material hierarchy model. Specific specialisation classes represent the classification of the materials (e.g., Solid class, Liquid class, etc.). All the materials can be characterised by the concentration of elements of which it is composed (ElementConcentration class), and a different set of technical properties (TechnicalProperty class) depending on the type of material (i.e., Solid , Liquid). Furthermore, each element concentration can be classified according to its type (ChemicalElementConcentation class, PolymenElementConcentation class, OtherElementConcentation class) and can play a different role (ElementRole class), such as basic, filler or additive.

In addition, each material can be identified based on a naming (MaterialNaming class) according to a specific standard (Standard class) and/or a trade name (MaterialTradename class) defined by the company supplying it (Company class) , as well as the description of its typical application(s) (MaterialApplication class).

Competency Questions

This section shows the specification of the requirements for the Material module in the form of key basic Competency Questions..

  • CQ.01: What is the composition (i.e., list of element concentration) of a material?
  • CQ.02: What is the concentration of basic/filler/additive elements in a material?
  • CQ.03: What materials contain an element?
  • CQ.04: What are the technical properties of a material?
  • CQ.05: What is the list of lubricants?
  • CQ.06: What are the materials of a material type (e.g., Steel, Oil)?
